## üìä MARKET MAKER SELL MODEL (MMSM)

### Overview
Blueprint for price delivery from **Bearish PD Array to Bullish PD Array**
Used when HTF structure is bearish, next draw on liquidity is lower

### Four Stages:

#### Stage 1: Original Consolidation (Accumulation)
- Price ranges between two limits
- Smart money building SHORT positions
- Retail traders accumulating longs at support

#### Stage 2: Engineering Liquidity (Manipulation Setup)
- Price makes HIGHER LOWS creating buy-side liquidity
- Each HL becomes stops for shorts / entries for breakout longs
- This is the "trap" being set

#### Stage 3: Smart Money Reversal (Judas/Manipulation)
- Price spikes ABOVE consolidation highs
- Sweeps buy-side liquidity (BSL)
- Triggers breakout buyers' entries and short sellers' stops
- THIS IS THE FALSE MOVE

#### Stage 4: Liquidity Hunt (Distribution/Expansion)
- Price reverses sharply DOWN
- Displacement through structure
- Targets the engineered liquidity (all those higher lows)
- Finally delivers to original consolidation low or beyond (SSL)

### Entry Logic:
- Wait for Stage 3 (liquidity sweep above)
- Look for MSS (Market Structure Shift) on LTF
- Enter on FVG/OB retracement that forms BELOW the swept liquidity
- Target: Sell-side liquidity (old lows, SSL pools)

## üìà MARKET MAKER BUY MODEL (MMBM)

### Overview
Blueprint for price delivery from **Bullish PD Array to Bearish PD Array**
Used when HTF structure is bullish, next draw on liquidity is higher

### Prerequisites:
1. HTF market structure = BULLISH
2. Next draw on liquidity = HIGHER
3. Sell program on LTF reaching into HTF bullish PD array

### Four Stages:

#### Stage 1: Original Consolidation (Accumulation)
- Price ranges between limits
- Smart money building LONG positions
- Retail selling at resistance

#### Stage 2: Engineering Liquidity
- Price makes LOWER HIGHS creating sell-side liquidity
- Each LH becomes stops for longs / entries for breakdown shorts
- Creating the trap

#### Stage 3: Smart Money Reversal (Judas/Manipulation)
- Price spikes BELOW consolidation lows
- Sweeps sell-side liquidity (SSL)
- Triggers breakdown sellers' entries and long holders' stops
- THE FALSE MOVE

#### Stage 4: Liquidity Hunt (Distribution/Expansion)
- Price reverses sharply UP
- Displacement through structure
- Runs through all engineered lower highs
- Delivers to original consolidation high or beyond (BSL)

### Entry Logic:
- Wait for Stage 3 (liquidity sweep below)
- Look for MSS (Market Structure Shift) on LTF
- Enter on FVG/OB retracement that forms ABOVE the swept liquidity
- Target: Buy-side liquidity (old highs, BSL pools)

## ‚ö° POWER OF THREE (AMD) - Daily Application

### The Three Phases:
1. **Accumulation**: Day opens, price ranges near open, SM builds positions
2. **Manipulation**: False move opposite to daily bias (Judas Swing)
3. **Distribution**: Real move delivering to daily target

### Bullish Day AMD:
- Accumulation near open
- Manipulation: Drops BELOW open, sweeps old lows (SSL)
- Distribution: Rallies UP, targets old highs (BSL)
- Daily candle = bullish body with wick below open

### Bearish Day AMD:
- Accumulation near open
- Manipulation: Spikes ABOVE open, sweeps old highs (BSL)
- Distribution: Sells off DOWN, targets old lows (SSL)
- Daily candle = bearish body with wick above open

## üîë KEY TRADING RULES

### What Constitutes a Valid Trade:
> "Look for FVG ABOVE the liquidity or BELOW it, so that way it has the target we're looking for."

Translation:
- **Sell Setup**: FVG forms ABOVE a liquidity pool (target is BELOW)
- **Buy Setup**: FVG forms BELOW a liquidity pool (target is ABOVE)
- The liquidity pool IS the target - if there's no draw on liquidity, there's no trade

### Timeframe Hierarchy:
- **15M**: Spot liquidity pools and model structure
- **5M or lower**: MSS confirmation and entry execution
- **HTF (4H/Daily)**: Confirm overall bias and model direction

### Entry Sequence:
1. Identify model (Buy or Sell) from HTF
2. Wait for manipulation phase (liquidity sweep)
3. Confirm MSS with displacement on LTF
4. Enter on FVG/OB retracement
5. Stop below/above last swing
6. Target opposite liquidity
