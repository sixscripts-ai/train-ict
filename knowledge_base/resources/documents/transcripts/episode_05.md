# Episode 5: Validation & Swing Identification

## No Trade Zone
- **12:00 PM - 1:00 PM EST**: Avoid trading. Lunch hour often has choppy/manipulated price action.

## Swing Highs/Lows
- **Identification**: Use the 15-minute chart.
- **Pattern**: A 3-candle formation (High with lower highs on immediate left and right).

## PM Session
- **Start Time**: 1:30 PM (13:30) EST.
- **Characteristics**: Often follows the AM trend or seeks liquidity left behind.

## Patterns
- **3 Drives Pattern**: Can validate a trade without taking out a major high/low if displacement is strong.
- **Displacement**: The non-negotiable factor. If price drifts, stay out.
